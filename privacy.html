Privacy

Last updated 2026.

This website

This site is a static marketing site — it does not use tracking cookies or analytics. The only personal data collected here is what you choose to submit through the contact form, which is delivered by email via FormSubmit.co directly to our team. We use it only to respond to your enquiry.

Our tools on Apify

Each tool listed on this site runs as an independent Actor on the Apify platform. Data you submit to a tool (e.g. a file, a URL, a name to screen) is processed for that run and is not shared between tools. Where a tool's product page states that nothing personal is stored beyond a run's own output, that means exactly that — no separate database of your inputs is kept by us. Apify's own platform-level terms and data handling apply to how runs are hosted; see Apify's legal pages for details.

Contact form (FormSubmit)

Our contact form submits directly to FormSubmit.co, a third-party form-delivery service, which forwards your message to our email address. We don't store submissions ourselves beyond our own email inbox. Do not include sensitive personal data in a contact message.

Your rights

If you'd like a copy of, or to have deleted, any personal data you've sent us via the contact form, email us and we'll action it. See Get in touch.

Changes

We may update this page as the site or our tools change. Material changes will update the "last updated" date above.
